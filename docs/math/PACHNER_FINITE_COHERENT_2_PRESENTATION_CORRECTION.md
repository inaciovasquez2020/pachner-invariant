For fixed dimension d and fixed PL manifold M, the Pachner move groupoid on triangulations of M admits a finite coherent 2-presentation.

Equivalently, there exist finite sets

- M_d: generating Pachner moves,
- R_d: generating relations among move words,
- C_d: generating coherences among relations,

such that

Pi_1(P_d(M)) = < M_d | R_d >

and every relation among elements of R_d is generated by C_d.
