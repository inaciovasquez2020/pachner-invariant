# G2 Construction Program

Status: Conditional

## Objective

Construct an explicit finite family G_2 of elementary d=2 flip-graph circuits.

## Canonical candidate classes

1. Pentagon circuit (associahedron 5-cycle).
2. Square commutation circuit from disjoint flips.
3. Triangle relabel / symmetry circuit.
4. Boundary transport circuit.
5. Finite relabel images of the above.

## Required theorem

Every closed flip loop γ decomposes as

γ = Σ_i g_i,   g_i ∈ G_2,

with each g_i supported on a patch of diameter at most C_2.

## Minimal executable route

1. Encode triangulated polygons.
2. Enumerate flip graph for n = 4,5,6,7.
3. Detect simple cycles.
4. Classify cycles into candidate classes.
5. Test whether all cycles are generated by candidate classes.
6. Infer stable finite family G_2.

## Upgrade gate

Do not upgrade status unless:
- explicit members of G_2 are listed,
- exhaustive finite checks are stored,
- decomposition theorem is formalized.
