## Elementary circuit family candidate

Define a finite family G_2 of elementary flip-graph circuits.

Required output:

1. Each circuit in G_2 is explicitly listed.

γ = Σ_i g_i,  g_i ∈ G_2,

with each g_i supported on a patch of diameter at most C_2.
